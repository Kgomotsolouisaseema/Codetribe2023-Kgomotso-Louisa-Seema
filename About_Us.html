Who am I ?

I’m a women on a mission ,I am the dreams and cries of those who came before me , I am Spirit , Body and Being. I am the first child of my mother and sister to a beautiful, wild flower also finding her way in this wilderness.
I grew up around the province of Gauteng but I’ve always wanted to travel the world.
Because of this desire of travelling, I asked mother to take me to a particular private school where I could study Hospitality and Catering , Computer Application Technology and Tourism.
This decision would till this day inform all my plans and direction for life and how I pursue it.
Because cooking was my favourite of all being that I love hosting people and having people enjoy my food ,I focused on it more . I went to college and studied the course and finally started travelling to USA. In the year I was an immigrant, I made the most of life and the possibilities of it. I enjoyed the hard work and long hours, the new scenery and visited other towns and met with the “locals”.
When the year was complete, I didn’t want to leave but I knew I couldn’t stay legally, I came back home to see family and friends and carry on my travels to other wonderful destinations or so I thought.
